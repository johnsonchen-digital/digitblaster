Digit Blaster

Retro LCD Calculator Shooter — Support

How to Play

Digits advance from right to left across the LCD display. Your base digit is on the far left.

AIM: Tap to cycle your base digit through 0–9.
FIRE: Tap when your base digit matches an approaching invader to destroy it.

Destroy all invaders before they reach your position. You have 3 lives. Survive 9 stages of increasing speed to win.

Scoring

Points depend on how far right the invader is when destroyed — earlier shots earn more points. Destroy consecutive invaders whose values sum to multiples of 10 to trigger a bonus UFO target worth 150 points.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The game feels too fast / too slow.

The game has 9 stages with progressively increasing speed, from approximately 6.6 seconds per screen crossing in Stage 1 down to 2.2 seconds in Stage 9. This is by design. Future updates may add difficulty settings.

Q: Does this app collect any data?

No. Digit Blaster runs entirely offline and collects no data whatsoever. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Which devices are supported?

Digit Blaster requires an iPhone running iOS 16.0 or later.
