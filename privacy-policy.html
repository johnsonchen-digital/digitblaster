Privacy Policy

Digit Blaster — Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Digit Blaster is a single-player offline game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles (or rather, does not handle) your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Digit Blaster does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, device identifiers, or any other data of any kind. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

Third-Party Services

Digit Blaster does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. The app contains no ads and makes no network requests.

Data Storage

Any game data (such as high scores) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted. This data is removed when you delete the app.

Children's Privacy

Digit Blaster does not collect any data from any users, including children. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
